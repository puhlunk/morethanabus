Our Mission

"To impact the needs of the hurting by bringing hope into cities where hunger and homelessness exists"

More Than a Bus is a non-profit 501c3 organization that serves the physical, emotional and spiritual needs of the homeless, poor, underserved, oppressed and mentally unstable persons (collectively the "hurting") in communities throughout Macomb, Oakland and Wayne Counties where homelessness and hunger exist.

We consistently provide food, personal hygiene items and articles of clothing to the hurting in those communities by traveling to the same location weekly. This is done primarily through the work of volunteers and funded through voluntary contributions from the general public and those persons interested in the goals and mission of More Than a Bus.

The Story

More Than a Bus was founded with a vision to serve communities throughout Macomb, Oakland and Wayne Counties where homelessness and hunger exist. We recognized that many individuals lack access to consistent, nutritious meals and supportive human connections.

Our organization converted a bus into a mobile kitchen and community center to bring resources directly to those in need. We don't just deliver foodâ€”we build relationships and restore dignity to those who are struggling.

By traveling to the same locations each week, we create a reliable presence that people can count on, fostering trust and enabling us to provide consistent support to those we serve.

The I.M.P.A.C.T Plan

The mission shall be carried out through the following means:

Immediate

To provide for immediate needs of the homeless and impoverished

Mobilize

To travel into areas of poverty and homelessness serving individuals and families

Partner

To collaborate with community partners in order to help people become self-sustaining through spiritual support, life skills, employment skills and peer mentoring

Authentic Relationships

To put God's love into action by valuing each person, yet holding them accountable and walking with them in their struggles

Consistency

To be a ministry that can be counted on by traveling to the same location weekly

Transform

To transform people's lives by improving their quality of life and allowing them to find the hope in Jesus
